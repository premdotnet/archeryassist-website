Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Archery Assist is a volunteer-run, non-commercial WhatsApp-based training log built for archers and coaches. We respect your privacy and aim to collect as little information as possible while providing useful training summaries and tools.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use Archery Assist:

  • Messages you send to the system (training logs, arrow counts, notes, simple commands).
  • Your WhatsApp phone number (required to interact with the bot).
  • Timestamps of messages for training history.

We do not intentionally collect:

  • Payment or financial information.
  • Your contact list.
  • Precise location data.
  • Advertising or tracking IDs.

3. How We Use the Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Record training sessions and arrow volume.
  • Provide daily, weekly, and monthly summaries.
  • Enable coaches (where configured) to view training patterns.
  • Maintain basic system performance and reliability.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent user data to third parties. Data is not shared with advertisers or data brokers. We may share limited information only if:

  • Required by law or a valid legal process, or
  • Needed to protect the integrity and security of the service.

5. Data Security

Data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure with reasonable technical and organizational measures. Access is restricted to designated volunteers responsible for maintaining the system.

6. Data Retention

Training logs may be retained as long as you or your club are actively using the service. If you stop using the system, logs may be kept for a period for analysis and support, unless you request deletion.

7. Your Choices & Deletion

You may request removal of your training history by contacting us. After verifying your identity, we will make reasonable efforts to delete associated records.

To request deletion or ask privacy-related questions, email:

info@archeryassist.prayas.info

8. Children’s Privacy

Archery Assist may be used by youth athletes, typically under coach and/or parent supervision. We encourage guardians to help younger athletes use the system appropriately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

10. Contact Us

For any privacy concerns or questions about this policy, please contact: info@archeryassist.prayas.info
