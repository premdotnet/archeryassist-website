Non-Commercial Use Statement

Archery Assist is a volunteer-run initiative created to support archers and coaches by simplifying the process of tracking training volume. It is not operated as a business and does not have a commercial model.

Key Points

  • No Fees: Users are not charged for using Archery Assist.
  • No Subscriptions: There are no subscription plans or premium tiers.
  • No Advertisements: The service does not display advertising.
  • No Data Selling: User data is not sold or shared with advertisers or data brokers.
  • Community-Focused: The goal is to help athletes and coaches improve training, not generate revenue.

Purpose

The purpose of Archery Assist is to make it easy for archers to log sessions and for coaches to see meaningful training information over time. It is designed as a tool for learning, performance improvement, and better communication inside the archery community.
