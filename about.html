About Archery Assist

Archery Assist is a volunteer-driven, non-commercial training support tool built for archers, coaches, and clubs. It was created to solve a simple but important problem: keeping a consistent record of training volume without adding more apps or complexity into the athlete’s life.

Mission

Our mission is to make training tracking effortless, accessible, and free for archers at every level—from beginners to international competitors.

What We Do

Archery Assist provides:

  • A WhatsApp-based interface to log arrows and sessions.
  • Summaries of daily, weekly, and monthly training volume.
  • Basic tools for coaches to see athlete commitment and preparation.
  • Support for competition and scoring-round tracking.

Who We Support

  • Youth archers who need encouragement and structure.
  • Competitive athletes targeting state, national, or international events.
  • Coaches managing individual athletes or squads.
  • Clubs & programs that want consistent training data.
  • Parents who wish to follow and support athlete progress.

Non-Commercial by Design

Archery Assist does not charge users, does not display advertisements, and does not sell user data. It exists solely as a community initiative to support better training habits and coach–athlete communication. For more details, please see the Non-Commercial Statement.
