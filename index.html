Archery Assist

WhatsApp-based training log for archers and coaches.

What is Archery Assist?

Archery Assist is a lightweight tool that lets archers log their daily training volume and key notes directly from WhatsApp — no app install, no spreadsheets, no extra friction.

Who is it for?

  • Archers who want to track arrows shot and sessions over time.
  • Coaches who want a simple view of athlete training volume.
  • Clubs that need a low-maintenance logging system.

How it works (high level)

  1. Archers join a WhatsApp number or group configured for Archery Assist.
  2. They send simple messages like: “120 arrows @ 70m, morning session”.
  3. The system stores and aggregates this data securely in the backend.
  4. Coaches can later view summaries and trends (per archer, per week, etc.).

More features will be added gradually (reports, summaries, reminders, and more).
