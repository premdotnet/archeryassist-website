Why Archery Assist Aid?

Consistent, measurable training is the foundation of high performance. Archery Assist Aid keeps the logging part effortless.

1. Natural WhatsApp Logging

Archers log sessions by sending simple messagesâ€”no special app or interface. The system parses arrow count, distance, and notes automatically.

2. Coach Tools

Coaches can review logs by athlete, view date-range summaries, and understand commitment and training levels across a squad, using simple WhatsApp commands.

3. Weekly & Monthly Summaries (coming soon)

Upcoming dashboards will show weekly and monthly arrow totals at a glance, helping athletes and coaches understand load patterns and training consistency over time.

4. Competition & Scoring Tracking

Competition rounds and scoring sessions can be logged, helping coaches analyze performance over timeâ€”not only practice volume.

5. Season Planning Support (coming soon)

Coaches will be able to define a preferred list of competitions and align training volume with the real calendar, helping athletes prepare smarter for key events.

6. Non-Commercial & Community-First

Archery Assist Aid is a volunteer project. There are no fees, no ads, and no data sellingâ€”only support for archers and coaches.
